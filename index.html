Enjoy a safer travelling experience with



Our social navigation app idea will allow users to have an anonymous account, navigate, and report inappropriate behavior in the user's provided area. Our goal is to promote public safety and awareness.

Special features included with your safety in mind.

Identity Verification

Accounts will be linked to real identities to minimize false reports and ensure accountability.

Navigation

Embedded Maps will allow easy navigation of street views and searching up locations.

Report

Users can submit reports on various categories of assault, including but not limited to verbal assault, physical assault, sexual assault, aggravated assault, etc.

View Reports

Users can view all reports, or reports in the user's area, filtered by date and public/private location.

Save Locations to Notify

Input a location and control whether to notify incident reports near the location.

Add Emergency Contacts

Users can request immediate police assistance or add the phone numbers of important people to call during emergencies.
